About Me

Meet Krisey D

Hello! I'm a versatile artist, chef, hairstylist, and devoted lover of God. Three years ago, amid the Pandemic, I discovered the therapeutic joy of painting. Now, with over 150 vibrant oil and acrylic pieces, my collection showcases cannabis art, abstracts, florals, and large wooden creations—all bursting with color, my true love. In the kitchen, I'm crafting culinary delights, and in the salon, I'm shaping self-expression through hairstyling. Join me on this colorful journey, where every stroke, dish, and haircut is a celebration. Welcome to my world!
